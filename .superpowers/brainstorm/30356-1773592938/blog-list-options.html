A — Magazine Grid

First post gets a large hero card (left half), next two share a column (right half), remaining posts fill a 3-column grid. Editorial, high-impact.

Pros

  • Featured post gets attention
  • Shows more posts above fold

Cons

  • Unequal post hierarchy
  • Most complex layout

B — Card Grid + Tag Filters ⭐ Recommended

Equal 3-column card grid with category filter pills at the top. Every post gets the same visual weight. Clean, modern, easy to scan.

Pros

  • All posts equally visible
  • Tag filtering adds discoverability
  • Scales well as posts grow

Cons

  • No featured post emphasis

C — Horizontal List Rows

Full-width horizontal rows — thumbnail left, category + title + excerpt middle, "Read →" right. Dense, scannable, information-forward.

Pros

  • Most content visible
  • Very easy to scan
  • Excerpt gives context fast

Cons

  • Less visual impact
  • Images are small
